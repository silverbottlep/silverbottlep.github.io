Course Description

This seminar-based course delves into cutting-edge developments in visual computing, focusing on recent advancements in visual computing and generative models. Students will engage with contemporary research papers, presenting and critically analyzing the methodologies, results, and implications of each study. Through active discussions, participants will enhance their understanding of complex visual computing concepts and explore their applications in real-world scenarios. The course fosters a collaborative learning environment, encouraging students to debate and challenge current trends and findings in the field.
